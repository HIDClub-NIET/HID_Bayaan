About HID

HID Club (Hope In Darkness) is a student-led initiative founded at Noida Institute of Engineering and Technology. We are dedicated to addressing the often overlooked aspects of mental health and personal development. Our mission is to break the silence, challenge stigmas, and create a safe space where everyone can openly discuss their mental well-being. We believe that healing begins with sharing our stories and embracing the challenges we face together.

What We Do?

Conversations

At HID Club, we believe in the power of sharing. Our platform encourages open dialogues about mental health, helping to reduce stigma and foster community understanding.

Workshops

Our workshops nurture personal development, equipping individuals with tools to manage stress and enhance mental health through mindfulness and self-care strategies.

Support

We create a safe space for members to connect and uplift one another. Through peer-led groups and activities, we build a community rooted in empathy and understanding.

Our Services

1 to 1 couselling

Our private, secure one-on-one counseling sessions provide personalized support to help you overcome challenges and enhance your wellbeing.

Group Sessions

Join our group counseling sessions to connect with others, share your story, and gain insights in a warm and supportive environment.

Online Sessions

Our virtual meetings provide comfort and assistance, allowing you to engage in deep discussions from the privacy of your own home

Workshops

Our workshops create chances to connect with like-minded individuals, gain new perspectives, and learn practical strategies for daily life.
